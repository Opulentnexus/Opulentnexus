5 Design Trends You Can’t Ignore

Stay ahead of the curve with these must-know visual and UX design trends.

1. Neumorphism

Combining minimalism and realism, neumorphism creates a soft, extruded plastic look using subtle shadows. It’s ideal for buttons and cards in modern interfaces.

2. Brutalist Typography

Big, bold, and unapologetic fonts are making a statement. This trend rejects the overly polished feel of traditional web design in favor of raw impact.

3. Dark Mode Everything

More users prefer darker UIs for both aesthetics and eye comfort. Ensure your design works seamlessly in both light and dark themes.

4. Motion Microinteractions

Subtle animations — like a button jiggle or hover glow — increase engagement without slowing down the user experience.

5. Glassmorphism

Blurred backgrounds and frosted-glass effects continue to evolve, giving depth and elegance to modern UI layouts, especially in cards and modals.
